Mouse Events

Mouse Events do something when pressed.

  1. Click Event(onclick)
  2. Mouse Down Event(onmousedown)
  3. Mouse UP Event(onmouseup)
  4. Mouse Over Event(onmouseover)
  5. Mouse Out Event(onmouseup)
  6. Double Click Event(ondblclick)
  7. Mouse Enter Event(onmouseenter)
  8. Mouse Leave Event(onmouseleave)
  9. Mouse Move Event(onmousemove)
  10. Right click Event (oncontextmenu)
